Best e-Signature Platforms for Estate Agents (2025)

Speed matters in estate agency — and the longer a deal sits unsigned, the more likely it is to fall through. That’s why more agents are moving to e-signature platforms to secure agreements fast and professionally.

What to Look For

  • Legally binding, GDPR-compliant signatures
  • Audit trails and document tracking
  • Ease of use for signers (no accounts needed)
  • Templates for repeated use (e.g. tenancy, offers)
  • Integration with CRMs or property platforms

Top Platforms in 2025

1. DocuSign

The industry standard. Trusted by corporates and independents alike. Excellent audit trails, secure storage, and integration with major CRMs. On the pricier side, but solid.

2. Adobe Acrobat Sign

Clean interface with strong compliance support. If you’re already in the Adobe ecosystem, it fits seamlessly. Supports custom branding and templates.

3. Signable (UK Based)

Fast, affordable, and fully UK-compliant. A great option for lettings teams and smaller branches. Easy-to-use dashboard and pay-as-you-go options available.

4. PandaDoc

Popular for its template creation and automation. Not just for signing — great for proposals and letters too. Works well for agencies that want a more dynamic experience.

5. HelloSign (by Dropbox)

Simple and quick for small teams. Integrated with Dropbox and Google Drive, making it a smart choice if you’re cloud-based already.

Where’s the Revenue?

Many of these platforms offer referral or affiliate programmes — including PandaDoc and Adobe. You can link directly in blog posts or resource pages to generate passive income.
