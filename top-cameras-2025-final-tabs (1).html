Practical shooting tips

  • Use a tripod and shoot slightly wider (16–20mm equivalent) for interiors.
  • Shoot in daylight; open blinds and turn on all practical lights.
  • Level the camera and keep verticals straight to avoid distortion.
  • Edit lightly: exposure, white balance, and straightening — avoid heavy filters.
